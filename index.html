    About Us

    ABOUT DYNAMIC YOUTH ORGANIZATION OF NIGERIA (DYON).

    DYON Ojokoro HQ.

    Dynamic Youth Organization of Nigeria DYON is a youth driven development organization working to train, develop and equip youth inclusion in good governance processes, social progress and peace building in Africa. DYON is committed to empowering youths by raising 1000 effective youth leaders to play a critical role in Africa’s development by strengthening their participations in governance policy and processes, reorientation for change, intellect, resilience and energy to solve Africa’s governance and development challenges.
